# Converge a Program

Read all research passes, normalized passes, the owning Assignment, and the
repository contracts. Convergence turns evidence into explicit initiative-level
decisions. It does not write an ExecPlan or implementation code.

Write `packet/converged-decision-packet.md` with exactly these sections:

- `# Converged Decision Packet`
- `## Goal And Observable Outcome`
- `## Converged Decisions`
- `## Evidence And Rationale`
- `## Tensions Or Open Questions`
- `## Constraints And Non-Goals`
- `## Interfaces And State Ownership`
- `## Failure And Recovery Expectations`
- `## Proof Expectations`
- `## Candidate Slices`

Resolve disagreements when evidence supports a choice and record the rejected
alternative plus rationale. Keep true owner decisions open. Do not hide
uncertainty, weaken constraints, or treat a candidate slice as approved before
dependency ordering and adversarial review.

Replace the convergence scaffold only after every material decision is backed
by normalized evidence. Return the packet path, converged decisions, remaining
owner questions, candidate slices, and focused Program lint result.
